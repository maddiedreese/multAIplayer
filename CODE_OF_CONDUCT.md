# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in multAIplayer a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respectful disagreement, constructive feedback, responsibility for mistakes, and attention to the community's best interests. Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or attention, trolling or insulting comments, harassment, publishing private information without permission, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate professionally.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately through the repository's private contact or the security-reporting mechanism in `SECURITY.md`; mark the report as a conduct report. Reports are reviewed promptly, kept confidential as far as practical, and handled proportionately. Reports involving the maintainer may instead use GitHub's private abuse-reporting process.

The maintainer may correct, warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participation. Retaliation is prohibited. Good-faith appeals may use the same private channel with the subject “conduct appeal.”
