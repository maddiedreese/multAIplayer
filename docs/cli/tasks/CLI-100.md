# CLI-100: Reconnect, replay, history, and crash recovery

State: waiting_for_owner_approval

## Outcome

Make room participation durable across relay disconnects, restarts, delayed
backlog, and process crashes without duplicate messages or execution.

## Allowed scope

- Client-core recovery, local encrypted history, replay watermarks, and tests.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Ordered replay is idempotent.
- [ ] Relay backlog is not treated as permanent history.
- [ ] Local history is bounded, encrypted, and atomically written.
- [ ] Logout, leave, and destructive forget have distinct semantics.
- [ ] Recovery failures are explicit and non-destructive.

## Verification

- Relay restart, client crash, delayed backlog, duplicate delivery, and corrupt
  local-state journeys.
